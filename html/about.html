About Indiefinder

Learn more about Indiefinder!

Our Mission

Indiefinder is dedicated to helping players discover and enjoy the best indie games available. We curate a diverse selection of games that showcase creativity, innovation, and unique gameplay experiences.

How It Works

Indie finder works very simply: we look for indie games personally recommended by you! We check to make sure they are appropriate for an average audience, which means no nudity or explicit content, then we check to make sure it's quality and engaging for players by checking reviews and even playing some ourselves. If the game meets our standards, we add it to the catalog and you'll see it on our game library soon after!

How You Can Help

If you have a favorite indie game that you think deserves more attention, please let us know! You can submit your recommendations through our contact page, and if we find that the game meets our criteria, we will add it to our catalog for everyone to discover and enjoy!

To submit a game recommendation, please visit our contact page and fill out the form with your suggestion.
